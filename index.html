Rules & Scoring

How to Play

How Much?

  1. Analyze the Matchup: Review the category (e.g., Career Pass Yards) and the two players displayed. Note if the prompt asks for the Higher or Lower stat.
  2. Pick the Winner: Tap the card of the player you believe is the statistical leader in that category.
  3. Estimate the Gap: Use the dual-slider controls to set a "Min" and "Max" range for the difference between the two players.
    • Example: If Player A has 50,000 Pass Yards and Player B has 45,000, the difference is 5,000. You might set your range from 4,000 to 6,000.
    • The tighter your range, the more points you score — but you risk missing the answer entirely.
  4. Lock In: Submit your guess to reveal the answer and score points.

Showdown

  1. The Face-Off: You are presented with two players and a list of 10 different statistical categories.
  2. Pick 10 Winners: For each row, tap the name of the player you think has the better stat in that specific category.
  3. Lock In: You must make a selection for all 10 categories before you can submit.
  4. The Reveal: The game reveals the actual stats row-by-row. You need at least 6 correct to score points, and 10/10 for a massive bonus.

Pick 'Em

  1. The Grid: You are given a stat category, a threshold (e.g., Career Rush Yards ≥ 10,000), and a grid of 10 players.
  2. Make Your Picks: Tap the players you believe meet the criteria. Each correct pick earns 100 points.
  3. Chain Bonus: If you pick players in order from farthest to closest to the threshold, you earn escalating bonus points (+50, +100, +150…).
  4. Be Careful: A wrong pick ends the round immediately. You can make up to 5 picks per round.
Scoring Breakdown

How Much? (Max 1,000 pts/round)

  • 1. Correct Player +200 Pts
  • You earn these points simply for identifying the correct winner. If you pick the wrong player, you score 0 for the round.
  • 2. Range Accuracy 0 to +800 Pts
  • Awarded only if the true difference falls between your Min and Max sliders. The score is calculated exponentially based on how narrow your range is compared to the safe zone. A very tight, correct range yields max points.
  • 3. Bullseye Bonus +100 Pts
  • A bonus awarded if the true difference lands in the bullseye zone — the center of your predicted range.

Showdown (Max 1,000 pts/round)

Points are awarded based on the total number of correct selections (out of 10):

10/10 Correct 1,000 Pts
9/10 Correct 700 Pts
8/10 Correct 450 Pts
7/10 Correct 250 Pts
6/10 Correct 100 Pts
0-5 Correct 0 Pts

Pick 'Em (Max 1,000+ pts/round)

  • 1. Correct Pick +100 Pts each
  • Each correct selection from the grid earns 100 points. You can make up to 5 picks per round.
  • 2. Chain Bonus +50, +100, +150…
  • Pick players in order from farthest to closest to the threshold. Each consecutive pick that is closer earns an escalating bonus (+50, +100, +150, etc.).
  • 3. Wrong Pick Ends Round
  • Selecting a player who does not meet the threshold immediately ends the round. Points earned so far in the round are kept.
Eligibility Criteria

Players are filtered by Eligibility Criteria and Player Pool, selected independently on the home screen.

1. Veteran

Designed to include recognizable players with significant careers.

  • QBs: 64+ career games started.
  • RBs: 750+ career rush attempts.
  • WRs: 250+ career receptions.
  • TEs: 150+ career receptions.

2. All-Pro

A more exclusive tier featuring elite contributors.

  • Career Approximate Value (AV) ≥ 80.

3. Hall of Fame

Only the all-time legends.

  • Career Approximate Value (AV) ≥ 130.

4. Era Selection

  • Modern (2000+): Players active from 2000 to present.
  • All Eras: Includes historical players back to the 1960s.
  • Specific Decade: Players active during the selected decade (e.g., 1990s).
Special Features
Reroll

One free skip per game. If you don't like the matchup, reroll it for a fresh one. Available once per game (not per round).

Pro-Football-Reference Links

After a round is locked in, player names become clickable links. Tap any player's name to visit their official Pro-Football-Reference page and verify their stats.

Dead Zone

When enabled (in Settings), a striped "invalid zone" appears at the left of the slider. This prevents trivially wide ranges from earning points.

Number Format

Toggle between decimal (.12) and whole number (123) display in Settings.

Game Modes

How Much?

Pick the player with the higher stat, then set a range for the difference. Tighter range = more points.

Showdown

Two players, 10 stat categories. Pick who wins each. Need 6+ correct to score (100–1000 pts).

Pick 'Em

A threshold is shown. From 10 players, pick those who meet it. Correct picks earn 100 pts + chain bonuses.

Eligibility Tiers

Veteran

Multi-year starters: QBs 64+ starts, RBs 750+ carries, WRs/TEs 250+ receptions.

All-Pro

Elite contributors: Career AV ≥ 80.

HOF

Legends only: Career AV ≥ 130 or position HOF thresholds.
